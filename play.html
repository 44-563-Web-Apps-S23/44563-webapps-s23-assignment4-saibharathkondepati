Sai Bharath Kondepati snips a play

Name :A DOLLAR
Author : David Pinski
HEROINE

[_To_ COMEDIAN.]

But you let go my hands.

COMEDIAN

I think it's you who are holding fast to mine.

HEROINE

Why should I be holding you? If you pick up the dollar, what is yours is mine, you know.

COMEDIAN

Then let go of my hand and I'll pick it up.

HEROINE

No, I'd rather pick it up myself.

COMEDIAN

I expected something like that from you.

HEROINE

[_Angrily._]

Let go of my hands, that's all.
